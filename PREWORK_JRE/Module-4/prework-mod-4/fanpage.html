Who are Earth, Wind & Fire?

Chicago, Illinois, 1969 - The greatest band to ever face this planet, Earth, Wind & Fire, was born. Known for some of their hit songs: "September", "Reasons", "Love's Holiday", "Fantasy", and many more, they have encapsulated all those who have heard their angelic voices for half a century!

Earth, Wind & Fire is an American band which shaped the genres of R&B, soul, Afro pop, disco, jazz, funk, dance, and many more. To this day, EW&F are still commonly regarded as one of the most successful and innovative music groups of all time.

P.S. Rest in peace, Maurice White, founder and leader of Earth, Wind & Fire.
